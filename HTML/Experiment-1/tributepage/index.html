About Hela

Hela OdinsdottirHela Odinsdottir, the feared Goddess of Death, was once Odin’s firstborn and the proud commander of Asgard’s armies. Her ambition, however, grew too strong—even for Odin. Cast out for her thirst for power, she eventually returned to take back what she believed was rightfully hers inThor: Ragnarok (2017).

Family:
Father: Odin
Brothers: Thor & Loki
Played By: Cate Blanchett

Powers and Abilities

  • Superhuman Strength
  • Immortality
  • Creates blades and weapons from thin air

Appearances

  • Thor: Ragnarok (2017) - Movie debut
  • Marvel Comics (1964–present) - Classic villain in Thor’s stories
  • Animated Shows & Games - Appears in many Marvel adaptations
